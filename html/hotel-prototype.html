The Velvet Oasis Hotel — UX Website Prototype

This high-fidelity Figma prototype was designed to reimagine a luxury hotel’s online experience, with a focus on modern aesthetics, intuitive navigation, and user-centered interaction. Targeting diverse audiences such as couples, business travelers, and event planners, the design merges sophistication with functionality.

From consistent sticky navigation and responsive layout to guided booking flows with real-time feedback, every screen was crafted to ensure a seamless and delightful user journey. The minimalist color palette and structured content layout were carefully selected to reflect the elegance and versatility expected from a premium hospitality brand.
